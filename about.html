About Moment Maker



At Moment Makers, we believe that every moment has the potential to be extraordinary. Our passion is capturing those fleeting instances and transforming them into timeless art. Whether it’s a candid smile, a breathtaking landscape, or an unforgettable event, we are here to preserve your memories in a way that tells your unique story.


Moment Makers was founded in 2003 with a vision to capture and celebrate the extraordinary in everyday moments. Our journey began with a simple idea: that every smile, every glance, and every fleeting moment holds a unique story worth preserving. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a dedicated team of artists and photographers, each bringing their own touch of creativity and passion to our work.


At Moment Makers, my mission has always been simple: to capture the beauty in life's most authentic moments. Photography isn't just about taking pictures; it's about telling a story, evoking emotions, and preserving memories that last a lifetime. I started this journey with the belief that every moment, big or small, deserves to be remembered and celebrated. What began as a personal passion has grown into a shared vision with a talented team of creatives who are equally dedicated to turning fleeting moments into timeless works of art. Thank you for trusting us to be a part of your story. I look forward to capturing many more moments with you.

Gokul Lal

Founder & CEO of Moment Maker

A Recall Of Achievements

Moment Makers was founded in 2003 with a simple yet powerful vision: to capture and celebrate the beauty in life’s most authentic moments. Since then, we have had the privilege of growing from a small, passionate team to a thriving community of creatives who share the same dedication to visual storytelling.Over the years, we’ve had the honor of being part of countless milestones—whether it’s capturing intimate family moments, documenting grand events, or collaborating with brands to bring their vision to life. Our work has been featured in numerous galleries, exhibitions, and media outlets, earning recognition for our unique blend of artistry and authenticity. Each achievement has been a testament to our belief that every story is worth telling and every moment is worth cherishing.


From our early days to where we stand now, we’ve continued to push creative boundaries and expand our expertise, all while staying true to our founding values of excellence, creativity, and heartfelt connection. As we look ahead, we are excited to create many more timeless memories with our clients, capturing their moments with the same passion and dedication that has brought us this far.
